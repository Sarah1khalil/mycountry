Tourist Destinations

  • Pyramids: The Pyramids of Giza are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, attracting tourists from around the globe.
  • Luxor and Aswan: These cities are home to many ancient temples, including the Karnak Temple and the Valley of the Kings.
  • Sharm El-Sheikh and Hurghada: Known for their beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and luxurious resorts.
  • Cairo: Cairo is home to the Egyptian Museum, which houses incredible ancient Egyptian artifacts, as well as the famous Khan El Khalili market.
