Language

The official language of Egypt is Arabic, but many Egyptians speak English, especially in major cities and tourist areas.

Essential Phrases:

  • Hello = Ahilan
  • Thank you = Shukran
  • How are you? = Izayak? (for males) / Izayeki? (for females)
  • Yes = Aywa
  • No = Laa

Climate

Egypt enjoys a hot, dry desert climate in most areas:

  • Winter: Mild temperatures in most regions, especially in Cairo and Alexandria.
  • Summer: Extremely hot in the interior regions like Cairo and Upper Egypt, while coastal cities like Alexandria and Sharm El-Sheikh have milder summer weather.
